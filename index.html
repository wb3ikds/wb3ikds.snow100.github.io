The Event / What is the Snow 100?

Facilitated by acclaimed corporate business trainer Michelle Snow, The Snow 100 is an exclusive network of businesses committed to strategic growth and partnership. Through continued business and team building education for both owners and employees, Michelle Snow transforms small businesses into growing, thriving companies with lasting impact.

Unlike most programs available in today's business world, we offer powerful seminars and training experiences that engage every member of your team, helping them recognize limiting mindsets and develop skills for maximizing positive impact both within your organization and as agents of your business within the market.
